Our Story
We met almost two years ago in a small town Valkeakoski in southern Finland. One evening we both decided to go to a party were we both didn't really feel like going to. It also happened that most of our friends cancelled going. Being friends of friends, we met at that party and decided to stick together. One month later after many late night conversations we had our first date. Since that day we hadn't spent a day apart.

Since Bart had to go back to the Netherlands in May, Polina decided to do a minor there and go with him. We moved in together to a student house in Eindhoven. In autumn 2017 we went to Paris on holidays. After a day of exploring the city, Bart asked Polina if she wanted to go to Pont Alexandre III. That is the place where Bart went down on his knee and asked if Polina wanted to be his wife. Polina gladly answered yes!

As a young couple, little did we know how much work it is to arrange a wedding. Somewhat more than a year later we managed to prepare everything. Now we are ready to have a beautiful March wedding.
Wedding Details
We have decided to go for a small wedding with the closest family members and friends. The event will be held in the beginning of March, 2019 in Wasvenboerderij, Eindhoven, The Netherlands.

The Wasvenboerderij, an organic farm in the north-east of Eindhoven was chosen, as we had found the area and the atmosphere there beautiful and, additionally, a natural and ecological aspect of living is important for us.
